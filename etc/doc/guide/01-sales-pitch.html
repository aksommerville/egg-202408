What is Egg good at?

  • Retro style sprite based games.
  • Point-and-click visual novels.
  • Very small deliverable size, and simple packaging.
  • Good performance on low-spec hardware.
  • Very portable.
  • Multiple languages, as long as they read left-to-right and don't require combining glyphs.

Unsupported features

  • Networking.
  • Recorded audio. You get synth only.
  • Filesystem access. You get a simple key/value store only, and the user can disable that too.

Questionable

  • 3D graphics. We do provide direct OpenGL ES access, but you have to work from scratch there.
  • Very large games. The entire ROM gets loaded at startup. Fine in normal cases, but if you really push it there could be performance issues.
  • High-level virtual languages (Python, Ruby, Javascript, ...). In theory, you could bring your own interpretter. But by that point, you're probably better off with a different platform.
